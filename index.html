The origin of breast cancer


Cancer is a disease caused by the rapid and disordered multiplication of abnormal cells, which form a tumor. It can occur in different structures of the body and, when it involves the cells of the mammary glands, it determines breast cancer, which presents itself in different forms, some restricted to the ducts and lobules involved in milk production, others more aggressive, which take all breast tissues and can spread throughout the body.
